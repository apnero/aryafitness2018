REVIEWS

Community fueled fitness

Empowering women

#aryagirls

Arya Fitness Studio is a women’s only studio that emphasizes the idea of having fun while getting fit! Arya’s unique selection of classes allows each individual a chance to better their lives and achieve all their fitness goals.

Whether if its hanging upside-down and wrapping your body in a silk hammock, bending and twisting your body gracefully around a circle in the sky during aerial hoop, learning intricate and mesmerizing moves on a chrome pole, or taking one of our fun and challenging fitness classes…we have a place for everyone in the studio.

Arya Fitness Studio promotes not only the building of muscles, but also self confidence, self love and friendships. This studio is designed to bring women together in an atmosphere of positivity, where each member shares in the desire to become healthier in body, mind and soul….

Female focused...Small Classes...Intimate Setting...Empowering and Elevating Your Soul!

About Arya

Fitness

We offer 19 weekly small group fitness classes that will help you achieve all your fitness goals while having a blast with the girls.

Pole

Pole fitness will bring your workout to the next level. Find your inner vixen, your strength, and feel empowered. Our pole classes use your bodies weight and resistance, so you will become stronger inside and out.

Aerial

Challenge your body by flying with us during aerial (lyra), hoop, and hammock. Discover why we say life is better upside down.

Youth

Our youth aerial program, now offering aerial hammock, hoop and summer camp, is designed to empower the next generation of girls, by promoting self-love in a challenging and safe environment.
