    Pole Fitness

    • Beginner Pole 1:

      The beginning stage of pole fitness. Your feet are still on the ground or hovering. You will learn the mechanics: different grips, walking around the pole, proximity to the pole, weight distribution, momentum and speed control. Our goal is to get you comfortable around the pole while conditioning your muscles to be able to advance. You will learn the basic spins and climbing. Pole Flow is a minimum of 12 classes series, after which members will be assessed by the instructor and either advance to the next level or continue in the current level. We want members to progress when they are ready. {This is a 12 week session class, meets once a week. Pre-Registration is required at the front desk}

    • Floor Play – Beg 1:

      Heel clacking, booty smacking, down to the ground floorwork. This class uses the floor and pole for low flow tricks and dance moves. You'll learn different ways to move between the floor and pole, floor based poses, and transitions around the base of the pole. Come enjoy new ways to experience pole and your own exotic soul. Each week will be different from the last, allowing new students to attend without the worry of missing a class. This class is for all levels. You do not need prior pole experience. Suggested attire for this class is any of the following: Leggings and any socks. Shorts and high socks. Knee pads (You will want to be able to slide around without your skin stopping you.) You may wear heels instead of socks if you wish, but one or the other is required. {This is a 8 week session class, meets once a week. Pre-Registration is required at the front desk}

    • Beginner Pole 2:

      Moving up in the pole world! This is the next step in Arya's Pole Fitness Classes. In this class you will perfect a variety of climbs and dismounts from the pole. You will also learn the beginnings of inversions and gain different grip strengths with your hands and thighs. Students will learn how to properly invert and dismount from an upside down position. Basic inversion tricks will also be taught and mastered before progressing to the next level. This class works on becoming comfortable up high on the pole as well as being upside down. Must complete Beginner {1} Pole before advancing to Beginner {2}.

    • Night Cap Pole - Beg 2:

      Let that inner vixen out! Each week work toward developing a slow and sexy routine for yourself or to bring home to someone else. Routines will include a combination of pole work and lap dance. Ladies are encouraged to dress up in something sexy and to wear shoes if desired. This class is a level 2, pole flow must be completed prior to taking this class. {This is a 8 week session class, meets once a week. Pre-Registration is required at the front desk}.

    • Pole Strength - Beg 2:

      On this class you will use the pole as a tool to exercise and gain strength for all pole classes. In this class you will use the pole and other equipment to tone your entire body. It's not recommended if you are looking to learn the basics of pole fitness since it does not teach the basics of pole {Pole Flow is the beginner pole class}.

    • Intermediate Pole 1:

      This class explores more technically challenging tricks both higher up and low on the pole. Students will continue to develop strength, endurance, and fluidity. Must complete beginner 1 & 2.

    • Pole Conditioning - Intermediate 1:

      Pole Conditioning takes pole fitness to a whole other level! This intense pole boot camp will have you engaging your arms, legs, and core non-stop, while building up your stamina on the pole! This class is for intermediate learners and higher.

    • Pole Spinner - Intermediate 1:

      This class is not for the faint at heart, definitely a huge challenge with great rewards on a student pole progression. This class is designed for the hardcore pole students that are interested in building their pole stamina and strength. Pole Powerhouse is constructed from a bootcamp perspective of pole. It is required that the student is completely comfortable inverting on their own. Training will include consecutive inversions and pole climbs. Students will benefit greatly from the strength and consistency that will be developed in this class.

    • Intermediate Pole 2:

      The next journey in our Intermediate pole level challenges our endurance and strength. Now that you have mastered climbing, are comfortable going upside down, and don't mind the massive bruises in your inner thighs, you're ready to take your pole learnings to a new height.

    • Advanced Pole:

      Infinite numbers of tricks and transitions, new tricks developing everyday! Advanced Pole is designed for the over achieving pole students who desire to push their strength and flexibility to maximum capacity. Taking notes from the pro’s social media accounts and bringing it to life here in Brookfield, CT. Students will not be disappointed with advanced inverted combinations learned in this class.

    • Polesque - Intermediate Pole 2:

      Polesque is a routine based class designated to one specific song which includes a video produced at the end of the session. This class is done in either heels or bare feet depending on the dynamic of the song selected. The routine includes a handspring combination, dance sequence, floor work, and spin/static pole combination. Routines are usually within 3-4 minutes. The hard work and development of the routine is a rewarding experience for the student. A new routine is produced every 8 weeks.
