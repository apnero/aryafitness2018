#aryagirls

Bulldog is fitness-focused yoga and great music in one sweaty, energetic, heart-pumping brew.

We are yoga, rebooted – free from chanting, free from instructions in Sanskrit, free from those uncomfortable moments.

Being bulldog is about enjoying the ride and noticing the little things that make our world special.

We bulldogs take risks, work to keep our body and brain healthy, and don’t take ourselves too seriously. We’re the first on the dance floor (even if there isn’t one) and embrace new adventures. We seize the day…and we sweat!

About Arya
 

Fitness

Set to calm yet energetic music (in true bulldog style), Activate allows you to work your entire body in a dynamic "slow flow" class.

Pole

This is your whole body workout for the day. Invigorate is a quick moving “power flow” class…it’s ambitious, stimulating and pretty darn aerobic.

Aerial

Exhilarate is our Invigorate Class “dialed up” for an extra cardio and strengthening challenge.

Community

Whether you’re new to yoga or looking for a refresher, bulldog basics is the perfect way to start getting your bulldog on.
