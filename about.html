About arya fitness

Arya Fitness Studio is a women’s only studio that emphasizes the idea of having fun while getting fit! Arya’s unique selection of classes allows each individual a chance to better their lives and achieve all their fitness goals.

Whether if its hanging upside-down and wrapping your body in a silk hammock, bending and twisting your body gracefully around a circle in the sky during aerial hoop, learning intricate and mesmerizing moves on a chrome pole, or taking one of our fun and challenging fitness classes…we have a place for everyone in the studio.

Arya Fitness Studio promotes not only the building of muscles, but also self confidence, self love and friendships. This studio is designed to bring women together in an atmosphere of positivity, where each member shares in the desire to become healthier in body, mind and soul….

We are the only female focused fitness studio in Fairfield County offering a variety of aerial arts classes! Fly your way to fitness with our Aerial Arts classes!

Studio Guidelines

We do our best to hold true to our guidelines so our studio runs smoothly, members know what to expect from us and also what we expect from our members.

  • NO PHONES

    You are here to work hard and have fun. Please minimize using your phone during classes. All aerial instructors allow time at the end of class for pictures.

  • STAY UP TO DATE

    It's important that your contact information (email and cell phone number especially) are always up to date in our Zen Planner software system so we can reach you if there is a change to a class or cancellation.

  • DON'T BE LATE FOR THE PARTY

    Make sure to arrive early or on time for class. If you are more than 5 minutes late you will not be allowed to enter class and you will not receive a class credit. This is for your safety and the respect of other members and your instructor.

    Please wait at the front desk area until it's time for your class.

    Be mindful of the classes going on when you walk by, especially the pole room.

  • TAKE CARE OF OUR HOME AWAY FROM HOME

    Please be respectful and clean up after yourself in the studio and bathroom.

    CUBBIES: Fitness Students please place your personal items in the cubbies in the front area. Pole Students please place your items in the cubbies in the pole room. No bags should be on the floor in the pole room specially.

    Outside shoes (winter/dirt/mud/rain) are NOT allowed in the fitness/pole rooms. Please remove it while standing on the carpet and place it on the provided shoe bins.

  • WE NEED TO KNOW ABOUT INJURIES

    Inform your instructor of any injuries or ailments before class begins. Feel free to ask any questions.

  • YOUR BLING

    Please remove ALL jewelry before you begin aerial class and wash off any lotion. No exceptions.

  • IT'S GETTING HOT IN HERE

    We definitely turn up the heat in this place so be kind and maintain daily hygiene. Sorry we have to say it! The thermostats are all programmed to come on and off as needed for classes. Please do not adjust them.

  • DAYCARE HOURS ARE...NONE

    We love your kiddies but we cannot have them sitting in the studio during class. We dont always have someone at the front desk, an kids can't sit alone there. We understand emergencies come up, if you absolutely got stuck last minute please contact Daniela (860-960-3535) to make sure it's ok to bring you little one to class.

  • SHOW UP. NO EXCUSES

    We know life happens, your car breaks down, your late from work, etc. Please respect that if for ANY REASON you do not show up for class or cancel within the 4 hour window, you will lose the session. The only exception is illness.

  • CANCELLATION POLICY

    - Our cancellation policy is 4 hours prior to class start. If you don't cancel your reservation within that time frame your account will be charged on the next business day, according to this policy:

    - Members with a monthly membership: $10 fee for a no-show if a class has a wait list. $5 fee if you were a no-show for a non-wait listed class.

    - Members with a class pack/single class: one class will be deducted from your pack for a no-show if a class.

    - If you were present in a class and received the no-show email by mistake, please don't panic. We will be checking each case individually.

  • WAITLIST POLICY

    If you add yourself to the waitlist you can be emailed up to 1 hour prior to the start time of class to be added in. Once you are added the cancellation policy applies. If you don't want to wait then it is your responsibility to remove yourself from the waitlist, or contact the studio as soon as you receive the email that you got in.

  • LIGHTS, CAMERA, ACTION

    We know you love to capture great moments via photo or video. You may only snap a photo at the end of class when the instructor allows. Please ensure you do not capture anyone else in the photo or video.

  • SIGN UP & COMMIT

    Reservations are required for ALL classes. All reservations can be made up to 15 days in advance. Our classes fill up quickly and we have limited space.

  • NO PARTY FOR 1

    Arya is about community, camaraderie and dancing with our friends. We want our class environment to be fun and exciting every time therefore we do not run classes for 1 person. Please make sure to pre register for classes so we know you are coming! If there's only one person signed up for a class, the instructor will cancel that class and you will be notified.

  • SNOW POLICY

    If you are registered for a class and we are expecting snow we will post on both facebook and instagram if we chose to cancel. For evening classes we will make the call by 3pm the same day. If we chose to run classes and you are registered you will be held accountable to the 4 hour cancellation policy. If you are unsure of the weather and don't want to commit then it is your responsibility to remove yourself from the class.

  • MEMBERSHIP TERMINATION

    All memberships are under a 12 month contrac. If you need to cancel your contract prior to the end of your term, we require written notice (email or in person) according to this policy: - 60 Days Notice: no fee - 30 Days Notice: $150 fee - No Notice: $300 fee

  • MEMBERSHIP HOLD

    You may place your membership on hold for up to 3 months only due to work or illness. You will be charged a $15 hold fee per month and the hold months will be added to the end of your contract.

  • DRAMA FREE ZONE

    We are here to have FUN! Leave your baggage at the door and bring your dancin' pants inside. Always practice within your own limits, non-judgmentally, and with an open mind.

    This is our {Happy Place}. We are here to {Empower} each other. Arya is a safe place where you can be {YOU}. We will not tolerate any gossip, cattiness, unfriendliness, judgement, rudeness, drama, or mean comments.

Frequently Asked Questions

We know you have questions about what to wear, what to bring, and what to expect. So we've put together these questions and answers for you.

What is the difference between Aerial Silks and Aerial Hammock?

Aerial Silks are connected in the middle of the fabric, at the top, with two separate pieces of fabric hanging down. Aerial Hammock is the same fabric, but the ENDS are tied at the top, making a natural 'seat' or sling allowing the performer to sit inside the fabric like a hammock, or sling depending on if the fabric is spread out or compressed. Hammocks are used for both Aerial Yoga and Aerial Hammock classes. Additionally the silks can be tied together creating a hammock effect in silks class. Here at Arya, we only offer Aerial Hammock due to our ceiling height.

What is Aerial Arts?

Aerial Arts is a broad term used to categorize many forms of aerial dance including, aerial silks, hammock (sling), lyra, corde lisse (rope), static trapeze, are a few of the more popular. However, aerialist love to create their own equipment!

Popularized by Cirque du Soleil, aerial arts are rapidly gaining popularity as a fun and unique fitness program that can be enjoyed in many different varieties and levels of intensity. Aerial arts used to be very limited, only being taught in "circus schools" but now it is being offered to the public as a fun and unique dance & fitness program. One of the newest adaptations being aerial yoga.

Our unique Aerial Arts program is intended to give both kids and adults the opportunity to experience aerial arts in a fun and safe environment under the supervision of our highly trained staff.

It can be approached as a hobby or as a passion. Our classes are structured so you can enjoy them once a week, or used as your main source of fitness (2-3 times a week). Cross-training in many different disciplines is highly encouraged! It keep things fun and fresh all while getting a great workout! We also have showcases each year (youth classes), so you can even create your own routine and perform for family and friends!

We offer pole fitness, lyra hoop and hammock as part of our aerial arts program. Our regular scheduled classes are for any female 18 and older. We offer youth classes that can be enjoyed by girls as young as 10.

What should I wear?

We want you to be comfortable. Please wear your normal workout attire, pants, crops, tank top, or T shirt. Please bring a pair of shorts as you will need those to learn certain moves.

Pole Attire - bring a pair of shorts or purchase a pair in studio. No shoes needed.

Hoop & Hammock Attire - Your knees need to be covered; wearing leggings or crops is best. Please trim your finger nails before class to prevent snags on the material.

All classes. Jewelry is NOT allowed. no rings, bracelets, big earrings.

Please wash off ALL body lotion before class. Any lotion on the skin can be a hinderance as you may feel slippery on the pole.

What do I wear on my feet for aerial classes?

Nothing! We dance barefoot so you don't need any special type of shoes.

Do I need to bring a water bottle?

If you want you can bring your own water bottle. We do have filtered water in the studio as well.

I'm not in "shape", can I still take class?

YES! You do not need to be in "shape" to take a class. We start all beginner level classes very basic. We include conditioning exercises as well to help you begin to build the strength needed to advance.

Will I go upside down in my first class?

No! Beginner classes teach the fundamentals not inversions. Students can expect to learn basic walks, basic ground moves, some floor work and a basic climb.

Am I too old to take these classes?

Not at all! We have students from all ages! One of the top pole dancers in our industry is a woman named Greta who is 65 years old!

Do I need to pre register for class or can I just show up?

We highly recommend all students pre register for class to ensure they have a spot. If you walk in we cannot guarantee you a spot in class. Also you will need an account in our Zen Planner system as a student so it's best you set it up yourself beforehand so you know how to manage it.

What if I'm running late to class?

For your safety and the respect of other students we do not allow students to enter class late. Please arrive on time for class.
